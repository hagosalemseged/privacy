# Ant B2B Supplier App – Account Deletion

Users of the Ant B2B Supplier App may request the deletion of their account and personal data at any time.

### How to Request Deletion

Send an email to **[anttechnology0@gmail.com](mailto:support@yourdomain.com)** with your registered phone number or email address and a request to delete your account.

### Data Deleted

Upon verification, we will delete:

* Account information
* Profile details
* Business information
* Product and inventory data associated with your account

### Data Retained

Some records, such as transaction history, payment records, and security logs, may be retained as required by law or for fraud prevention purposes.

### Retention Period

Retained records may be kept for up to 7 years or as required by applicable laws.
