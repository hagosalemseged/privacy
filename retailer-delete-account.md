ANT Retailer – Account Deletion Request

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

Request Account Deletion

At ANT Retailer, we respect your privacy and provide users with the ability to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion using one of the following methods:

Option 1: Through the Mobile Application
Log in to your ANT Retailer account.
Navigate to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
Follow the instructions to confirm the deletion request.
Option 2: Contact Customer Support

If you are unable to access your account, you may submit a deletion request by contacting our support team:

Email: support@yourdomain.com

Please include:

Your registered phone number
Your account name (if applicable)
The reason for the deletion request (optional)
What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon approval of your deletion request, we will delete:

Profile information
Contact information
Login credentials
Saved addresses
Account preferences
Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained for legal, regulatory, security, fraud prevention, accounting, or dispute resolution purposes, including:

Transaction records
Payment records
Order history required by law
Audit and security logs
Retention Period

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days of verification. Any retained information will be stored only for the period required by applicable laws and regulations.
